About Game

What is our game?

Developed by Aston Computer Entertainment (ACE), Anya and Zuzannah is a turn based role playing game. Anya, a Russian scientist searching for artefacts in Angel Falls, Venezuela finds an ancient rock made of mankind’s rarest material; unobtanium; with properties that enhances the physical abilities of the wielder. Anya attracts the attention of “The Bandidos Del Caos”, who seek the unobtanium for their own purposes. Alongside Zuzannah, a rebel with a grudge against the Bandidos, Anya must fight against bandit and wildlife alike to escape Angel Falls and to deliver the unobtanium to Gemini Corporation, who promise a handsome award. As Anya progresses throughout the adventure, she’ll discover the true motives of those around her, as well as learn about the origin of the ubobtanium. Have you got what it takes?
